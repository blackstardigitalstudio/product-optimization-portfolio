## Client Digital Ecosystems

### Il Raviolo
Italian restaurant in Las Palmas. Full digital ecosystem: website, local SEO, booking UX, Instagram management.

**My role:** UX analysis · conversion direction · local SEO · social system
**Problem solved:** Tourist and local visitors weren't converting into bookings. Mobile experience had too much friction.
**Live:** [ilraviolo.es](https://ilraviolo.es)

---

### Gas Project
Automotive workshop · Italy. Institutional website and brand positioning.

**My role:** Product strategy · UX/UI · branding · lead generation system
**Problem solved:** No clear value proposition and no lead capture mechanism.
**Live:** [gasproject.it](https://gasproject.it)

---

### Mundo Imposible
New tourist attraction in Las Palmas. Conversion-first landing page.

**My role:** Conversion UX · mobile-first design · microcopy · booking flow
**Problem solved:** New concept that needed to explain itself in 3 seconds and convert curious visitors to bookings.
**Live:** [mundoimposible.es](https://mundoimposible.es)

## Independent Digital Products

### Il Ricettario Di Matteo
**Type:** Mobile app · **Stack:** TypeScript, AI, Node.js

Import recipe videos from Instagram/Facebook → AI extracts ingredients and quantities → organised private recipe book with folders, PDF export and sharing.

**Product logic tested:** AI extraction pipeline, content organisation, smart folder UX, cross-language support (IT/ES)
**Repo:** [il-ricettario-di](https://github.com/blackstardigitalstudio/il-ricettario-di)

---

### Lo Sapevi Che
**Type:** Mobile/web platform · **Stack:** Python, notifications, gamification

Gamified curiosity platform with interest categories, levels, progression, badges and daily engagement loop.

**Product logic tested:** Retention mechanics, notification strategy, gamification design, personalisation
**Repo:** [lo-sapevi-che](https://github.com/blackstardigitalstudio/lo-sapevi-che)

---

### Accadde Oggi
**Type:** Mobile app · **Stack:** TypeScript, smart notifications

Daily historical events discovery with smart notification scheduling, interest filters and content personalisation.

**Product logic tested:** Daily engagement loop, notification optimisation, content discovery UX
**Repo:** [accadde-oggi](https://github.com/blackstardigitalstudio/accadde-oggi)
